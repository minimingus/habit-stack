# Better You - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 20, 2026

Better You ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Account Data
- Email address (for authentication)
- Profile information you provide (display name)

### Habit Data
- Habits you create (names, schedules, categories)
- Daily completion logs
- Streak and progress data

### Coach Conversations
- Messages you send to the AI Coach
- AI Coach responses
- Conversation history is retained to provide context for future coaching

### Analytics Data
- App usage patterns (screens viewed, features used)
- Onboarding completion status
- Anonymized engagement metrics (habit completion rates, streak milestones)
- Device type and OS version

### Purchase Data
- Subscription status (free or Pro)
- Purchase history is managed by Apple and RevenueCat; we do not store payment details

## How We Use Your Data

- **Provide the service:** Store your habits, track progress, and deliver AI coaching
- **Improve the app:** Analyze anonymized usage patterns to identify areas for improvement
- **Personalize your experience:** Tailor habit suggestions based on your selected identity traits
- **Enforce service limits:** Track usage against free-tier and Pro-tier limits

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Supabase | Authentication, database, backend | [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy) |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) |
| PostHog | Product analytics | [posthog.com/privacy](https://posthog.com/privacy) |
| Anthropic (Claude) | AI Coach (via server-side API) | [anthropic.com/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy) |

Your coach conversations are sent to Anthropic's Claude API via our secure backend. We do not share your personal data with Anthropic beyond what is necessary to generate coaching responses.

## Data Retention

- Account and habit data is retained as long as your account is active
- Coach conversation history is retained for up to 50 messages to provide context
- Analytics data is retained in anonymized form
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings

## Your Rights

- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all data via Settings > Delete Account
- **Portability:** Request your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out of analytics:** Contact us to opt out of PostHog analytics

## Data Security

- All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
- Database access is protected by row-level security (RLS) — users can only access their own data
- API keys and secrets are stored securely and never exposed to the client
- Coach conversations are processed server-side; your messages are not sent directly from your device to third-party AI services

## Children's Privacy

Better You is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
